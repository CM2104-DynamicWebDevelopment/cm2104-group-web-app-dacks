31st December 2019

First cases of a flu like illness have been reported in Wuhan.

1st January 2020

More flu like cases are reported, and the source of the illness is thought to be a food market in the centre of Wuhan.

5th January 2020

The World Health Organisation advises on a complete travel ban surrounding Wuhan.

7th January 2020

Virus is given its official name Coronavirus as bird flu and SARS are ruled out.
